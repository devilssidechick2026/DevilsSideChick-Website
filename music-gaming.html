Music & Gaming

Music

Music has always been another form of storytelling for me. Some stories become novels, while others become songs.

I write songs inspired by personal experiences, emotions, and the stories that stay with me long after they happen. Many of my songs are rooted not only in my own experiences, but also in the experiences of those closest to me. Grief, resilience, love, loss, faith, and survival often find their way into my lyrics because they are themes I understand deeply.

While I currently use Suno to give those songs a voice, my hope is to one day collaborate with artists who connect with the lyrics and want to make them their own. Every song begins with a story, and every story begins with a feeling that refuses to be ignored.

At the heart of every song is a story, and storytelling has always been where I feel most at home.

Listen to my music on Spotify, Apple Music, Amazon Music, YouTube Music, Pandora, and other major streaming platforms.

Gaming

I originally created Devils Side Chick as a way to be part of the gaming community while still keeping my privacy. I never expected it to become the name behind my books, music, and creative projects, but over the years it grew into exactly that.

Gaming introduced me to people I never would have met otherwise. I've made friends from different countries and cultures, including my best friend, Morgan, from England. Morgan actually inspired the British character Thorne in The Withered Rose. Some of those friendships have lasted longer than I ever expected and have become a meaningful part of my life.

For me, gaming has never just been about the games. It's about the people, the stories, and the communities built along the way.
