About the Author

Writing as Devils Side Chick, Brittany creates dark, emotional fiction about survival, betrayal, rebirth, and the kind of women who learn to turn pain into power.

Her stories blend gothic atmosphere, romance, supernatural danger, and deeply personal emotion. Her creative world also stretches into music and gaming, building a brand that is feminine, dangerous, and unapologetically alive.
