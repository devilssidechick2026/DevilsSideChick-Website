About the Author

Writing as Devils Side Chick, Brittany creates dark fiction where beauty rots beneath the surface, grief becomes a weapon, and survival often demands a terrible price. Her stories explore broken heroines, haunted towns, ancient secrets, and the darkness that lingers long after the last page is turned.

Blending gothic horror, dark fantasy, supernatural suspense, and emotional storytelling, her work follows characters who are forced to walk through the ashes of tragedy before discovering who they truly are. Every scar carries a story. Every monster wears a mask. And not every villain hides in the shadows.

Current projects include The Withered Rose and Devils Side Chick, two interconnected stories centered on loss, rebirth, power, and the dangerous things waiting beyond the veil.
