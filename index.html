Books

My stories are for readers who enjoy emotional stories that leave a mark, strong female protagonists, betrayal, resilience, and rebirth. My books explore love, loss, family, and survival through characters who are forced to face the impossible. Not every ending is a happily ever after, but every scar has a story.

Music

I write songs inspired by personal experiences, emotions, and the stories that stay with me long after they happen. Many of my songs are rooted not only in my own experiences, but also in the experiences of those closest to me. While I currently use Suno to give those songs a voice, my hope is to one day collaborate with artists who connect with the lyrics and want to make them their own.

At the heart of every song is a story, and storytelling has always been where I feel most at home.

Gaming

Gaming introduced me to people, places, and friendships I never would have experienced otherwise. What began as a hobby eventually became the foundation for the Devils Side Chick brand and connected me with people from different cultures around the world.
