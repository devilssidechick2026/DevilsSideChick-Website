About Me

Hi, I'm Brittany, though most people online know me as Devils Side Chick.

I didn’t start writing because I wanted to be an author. I started writing because I needed someone to listen.

For me, that someone was paper.

Paper doesn’t judge me. It doesn’t invalidate my feelings. It doesn’t argue. It doesn’t play devil’s advocate. It simply listens. It catches my tears, absorbs my frustrations, and quietly holds the words I don’t always know how to say out loud.

Journaling became my safe place, and somewhere along the way, those pages became stories.

I wanted to tell my story, but some stories aren’t easy to tell directly. Growing up in a well-known family, there were things I felt I couldn’t say. I learned to swallow my feelings, push through the pain, and never let anyone see me break. I was told to be stronger. So I found another way.

I created worlds.

The characters may be fictional, but the emotions are real. The grief, the anger, the hope, the fear, the resilience, I’ve felt them all. My stories and I share the same DNA. We simply traveled different roads to reach the same destination.

Today, I tell stories through books, music, and gaming communities. Whether I’m writing a novel, crafting song lyrics, or sharing adventures with friends online, everything I create begins the same way it always has:

With a story that needs to be told.

Thank you for being here and taking part in that journey.

Current projects include The Withered Rose and Devils Side Chick, two interconnected stories centered on loss, rebirth, power, and revenge.
