Meet the Author

Brittany is currently pursuing a Bachelor of Arts in Creative Writing at Southern New Hampshire University. Through her coursework, she has developed skills in literary analysis, storytelling, character development, and author platform creation.

She writes dark fantasy and horror for readers who enjoy supernatural worlds, emotionally complex characters, and stories of resilience, survival, and rebirth.

Although most people online know me as Devils Side Chick.

She writes under the pen name Devils Side Chick, a creative identity that allows her to maintain a degree of privacy while pursuing her passion for storytelling. After spending five years in law enforcement, she became increasingly aware of the importance of separating her professional life from her creative work. The pen name gives her the freedom to explore darker themes, supernatural worlds, and emotionally complex characters while building a brand that reflects the stories she loves to tell.

Brittany didn’t start writing because she wanted to be an author. She started writing because she needed someone to listen.

For her, that someone was paper.

Paper did not judge. It did not invalidate her feelings. It did not argue. It did not play devil’s advocate. It simply listened. It caught her tears, absorbed her frustrations, and quietly held the words she did not always know how to say out loud.

Journaling became her safe place, and somewhere along the way, those pages became stories.

She wanted to tell her story, but some stories aren’t easy to tell directly. Growing up in a well-known family, there were things she felt that she couldn’t say. She learned to swallow her feelings, push through the pain, and never let anyone see her break. She was told to be stronger. So, she found another way.

She began created worlds.

The characters may be fictional, but the emotions are real. The grief, the anger, the hope, the fear, the resilience, she has felt them all. Her stories and her share the same DNA. They simply traveled different roads to reach the same destination.

Today, she tells stories through books, music, and gaming communities. Whether she is writing a novel, crafting song lyrics, or sharing adventures with friends online, everything she create begins the same way it always has:

With a story that needs to be told.

Thank you for being here and taking part in that journey.

Brittany’s goal is to publishThe Withered Rose and Devils Side Chick, while continuing to grow her audience and establish herself within the dark fantasy and horror genres. She is currently building her author platform and preparing her work for future publication opportunities.
